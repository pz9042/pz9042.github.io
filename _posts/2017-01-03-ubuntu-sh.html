Ubuntu sh文件带[[]]报错问题

pengzhen@pz-test:~$ cat tmp.sh
#!/bin/bash
strA="helloworld"
strB="low"
if [[ $strA =~ $strB ]]
then
  echo "包含"
else
  echo "不包含"
fi
pengzhen@pz-test:~$ sh tmp.sh
tmp.sh: 4: tmp.sh: [[: not found
不包含
pengzhen@pz-test:~$ chmod u+x tmp.sh
pengzhen@pz-test:~$ ./tmp.sh
包含
